INTRODUCTION
Endometriosis is a condition when patients hace endometrial-type tissue outside of the utherus. It is a disease of adolescents and reproductive-aged women. Women with endometriosis are more likely to have painful mestruation and infertility or difficulty getting pregnant.

SYMPTOMS
-Pain
-Extreme menstrual cramps
